We warmly welcome contributions from the community to help improve the Jumper documentation and its core projects. Whether it's fixing typos, enhancing existing content, or adding new sections, **your input is highly valuable!**

Jumper and Carrot are open-source projects, and we firmly believe that collaborative efforts lead to better outcomes for everyone in the community.

## How to Get Started

To contribute to the Jumper ecosystem, please refer to the detailed guidelines in the `CONTRIBUTING` files on GitHub for each specific repository:

* **Contribute to the [Jumper Client](https://github.com/Jumper-Carrot/Jumper?tab=contributing-ov-file)**
    

* **Contribute to the [Carrot Server](https://github.com/Jumper-Carrot/Carrot?tab=contributing-ov-file)**
    
* **Contribute to the [Jumper Documentation](https://github.com/Jumper-Carrot/Jumper-doc?tab=contributing-ov-file)**
